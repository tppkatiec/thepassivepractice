The Three Doors

Three types of people land on this site. Every page decision is made with all three in mind. Approve this framework before anything gets built.

Door One
I need help with my relationship
Already tried therapy. Found Stephanie on Instagram or Google. Something landed. Needs a clear path from the homepage to the course, then the retreat.
Homepage → Quiz → Course → Retreat
Door Two
I want to work with her directly
High-ticket. Has done their research. Needs a direct path to the retreat or private sessions without being buried in course content.

Section 08
How the Copy Gets Written

Approve the process before writing begins.

The process
All copy is drafted using the Brand Voice Guide, the story bank of real client moments, and Stephanie's actual phrases. AI is used as a drafting tool only - never a replacement for voice or judgment. Every draft is built from her words.
  • Amanda drafts each page using the voice guide as the foundation
  • Draft delivered in Google Doc format - mark up directly in the doc
  • Stephanie flags anything that does not sound like her
  • One round of revisions per batch, included
  • Nothing gets published without her eyes on it
Priority order
Pages get written in this order, driven by revenue impact. Batch 1 gets approved before Batch 2 begins.
  • Batch 1: Homepage, About, Course sales page, all three Retreat pages
  • Batch 2: Whole Person Approach, all 8 specialty pages, Contact
  • Batch 3: Testimonials, Press and Media, Favorites and Shop, Blog
  • SEO: Keywords assigned before writing. Built in naturally, not stuffed.
  • URL redirects: Built before any pages go live. SEO authority protected.
