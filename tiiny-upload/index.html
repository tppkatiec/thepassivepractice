The Passive Practice / Brand Discovery

Before I design your brand,
I need to know you.

This is not a typical questionnaire. There are no right answers. The questions that make you think the hardest are usually the most useful. Be honest, be specific, and take your time with this.

4 sections  ·  about 15 minutes  ·  completely yours

01 / Your Practice

Let's start with you.

Q01
What's your name, and what do you call your practice?
Q02
In one sentence, what do you actually do?
Not your elevator pitch. The real version. What do you actually do day to day.
Q03
Who is this work for, really?
Not a demographic. The person. What's going on in their life when they find you.
02 / Your Voice
